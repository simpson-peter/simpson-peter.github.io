Peter Simpson CSCI 104 Final Project Summary

What is my final project?

My final project is a general-purpose personal landing page for those seeking information about me as a student and professional.

What does it accomplish for visitors?

  • Introduces me and my interests in a succint and professional manner.
  • Serves as a launching pad for direct contact.
  • Displays my resume for interested parties.
  • Demonstrates my experience through projects.
  • Serves as a central directory to organize the various projects I've pursued.

What extras did I employ to accomplish these goals?

Iframes
  • I utilized an iframe to display one of my vide-focused academic projects.
HTML5 Media Tags
  • I used a media tag (specifically audio) to showcase the guitar work I've done at USC. I utilized the additional controls attribute to allow the user to interact with the underlying audio and play/pause it at will.
